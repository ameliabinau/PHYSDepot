Astrophysics Starterpacks & Toolboxes 🔧

This page will provide information for those interested in exploring a specific subfield of astrophysics (or for anyone simply curious about how other fields approach their work). Each subfield will be broken into two "packages": starterpacks and toolboxes. Starterpacks work on the educational front: they contain suggested readings, interactives, lectures, or websites recommended by those working actively in the field to learn fundemental theoretical background in that subfield. Toolkits contain computational tools, software, datasets, and more necessary to pursue a research-based or computational project relevant to that field - these will be tools foundational to modern scientific research in that subfield.
