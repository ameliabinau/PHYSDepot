Usage Requirements: Internet Connection

NotebookLM is an AI Research tool developed by Google which can summarize, make connections, and answer questions from uploaded PDFs, websites, YouTube videos, audio files, Google Docs, and Google Slides.

Robert C. Laughlin, Indiana University - Bloomington

Usage Requirements: Internet Connection

SciSpace is an AI specifically for locating relevant articles for research topics. Use SciSpace to help speed up the process of finding relevant articles, it's like a more direct Google Scholar. SciSpace really helps with finding papers to read if you're getting into a field or doing a literature review.

Jonathan Sullivan-Wood

Usage Requirements: Internet Connection

W3Schools is a useful website to learn a huge variety of programming languages, ranging from Java, C, C++, html, css, SQL, Python and some of its commonly used libraries, and more.

Akshith Karri, Purdue University

Usage Requirements: Internet Connection

Famously hosting over 6,800 acres of land maintained to maximize biodiversity and benefit ecological conservation efforts, Fermilab National Accelerator Labortory harbors a handful of frog populations. While no longer maintained and only available thanks to the Wayback Machine, Frogs of Fermilab is a website where curious amphibian lovers can learn about the species they might encounter during an outdoor Fermiadventure.

Contributor: Hannah LeMoine, Indiana University - Bloomington
