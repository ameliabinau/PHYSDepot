NotebookLM is an AI Research tool developed by Google which can summarize, make connections, and answer questions from uploaded PDFs, websites, YouTube videos, audio files, Google Docs, and Google Slides.

Robert C. Laughlin, Indiana University - Bloomington

Usage Requirements: Internet Connection

SciSpace is an AI specifically for locating relevant articles for research topics. Use SciSpace to help speed up the process of finding relevant articles, it's like a more direct Google Scholar. SciSpace really helps with finding papers to read if you're getting into a field or doing a literature review.

Jonathan Sullivan-Wood
