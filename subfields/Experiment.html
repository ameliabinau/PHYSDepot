Usage Requirements: Windows, Mac, Linux, or Docker OS

Possibly one of the most commonly used software for designing printed circuit boards (PCBs) for electronics. Design a PCB board as a .kicad_pcb file and an associated schematic (quite literally a circuit diagram) as a .kicad_sch file, export as a Gerber file, and have your circuit printed by a reliable company such as PCBWay. When laboratories need a circuit to accomplish a specific digital or mechanical task, it's common to have scientists design their own on software such as KiCad. A similar software produced by Autodesk is EAGLE.

Contibutor: Amelia Binau, Indiana University - Bloomington
