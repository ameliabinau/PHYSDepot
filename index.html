Welcome!

PHYSDepot is a repository of physics resources helpful to physicists at all stages — from beginners to professional researchers. Our goal is to curate tools, packages, simulations, and educational materials that lower the barrier to entry and empower both students and scientists alike.

Whether you are just beginning your journey or working on advanced research, PHYSDepot is built to support your work. This platform was inspired by the struggle many scientists face when access to mentorship and educational opportunities is limited. We aim to connect, unify, and enable exploration through science and learning.
